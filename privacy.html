Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ColdPlungeAutoTimer ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our Apple Watch app. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

ColdPlungeAutoTimer does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data generated by the app — including session history, temperature readings, streaks, and settings — is stored locally on your device and Apple Watch only.

Health & Sensor Data

The app uses your Apple Watch sensors to detect water temperature in order to trigger the auto-start timer. This data is:

  • Processed entirely on-device
  • Never transmitted to any server
  • Never shared with third parties

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your Apple Watch and iPhone. We do not operate any servers or databases that store your information.

Third-Party Services

ColdPlungeAutoTimer does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
