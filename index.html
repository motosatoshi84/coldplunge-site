How to use ColdPlunge

Get started in under a minute.

1

Download the app

Install ColdPlunge from the App Store on your iPhone or iPad.

2

Set your goal

Choose a target duration for your plunge — start with 1–2 minutes and build from there.

3

Complete your breathing exercise

Follow the customizable guided breathing sequence to prepare your body and calm your mind before entering the water.

4

Get in — the timer starts itself

Once the water temperature drops below your preset threshold, ColdPlunge automatically starts your timer. No tapping required — just get in and breathe.

5

Track your progress

Visit the History tab to review your sessions, streaks, and trends over time.
